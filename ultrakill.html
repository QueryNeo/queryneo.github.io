WHAT IS ULTRAKILL?


ULTRAKILL is a fast-paced ultraviolent old school first-person shooter developed by Arsi "Hakita" Patala and published by New Blood Interactive.

It's a very technical, style-based shooter like the Devil May Cry series and also hails homage to retro shooters like Doom and Quake in its artstyle. You play as V1, a robot who dives into hell to take the blood of his enemies as fuel to power himself; and faces powerful angels and demons alike.
